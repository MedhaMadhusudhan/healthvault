About HealthVault

Our Mission

To build a secure, user-friendly web application enabling doctors and patients to manage medical reports and prescriptions, improving communication and record-keeping in healthcare. HealthVault aims to streamline the healthcare experience by providing a centralized platform for managing medical information.

Key Features

Secure Login System

Separate login portals for doctors and patients, ensuring appropriate access control and data privacy. Users can safely register, login, and manage their medical information.

Medical Record Management

Upload and manage medical documents, test reports, and prescriptions through an intuitive dashboard interface. Organized storage and easy retrieval of important health records.

Doctor-Patient Interaction

Enables doctors to access patient records, review uploaded documents, and provide guidance based on medical history. Streamlines communication between healthcare providers and patients.

How It Works

1. User Registration

Register as either a patient or doctor through our secure portal, providing necessary details and credentials.

2. Document Management

Upload and organize medical documents, test reports, and prescriptions through your personalized dashboard.

3. Healthcare Collaboration

Doctors can access patient records and provide guidance, creating a seamless flow of information between healthcare providers and patients.
